Biography

A Visionary Leader for Zimbabwe's Digital Future

Hon. Tatenda Mavetera is a dynamic leader and the current Minister of Information, Communication Technology, Postal and Courier Services for the Republic of Zimbabwe. A champion for youth and women's empowerment, she serves as the Member of Parliament for Chikomba West. With a strong background in strategic marketing and business leadership, Hon. Mavetera is driving Zimbabwe's digital future while fostering inclusive economic growth through her role as the Founding Chairman of Young Women for Economic Development.

Her journey from a prominent actress to a pivotal political figure demonstrates her multifaceted talent and dedication to public service. Currently pursuing a Doctorate in Business Leadership, she combines academic rigor with practical governance experience.
